Key Guests

Swami Mukundananda

Swami Mukundananda is an international authority on mind management and a unique saint with a distinguished educational background. He received his degrees from two world-renowned institutes, IIT and IIM. He left a promising professional career at the tender age of 23 to dedicate himself fully to the spiritual and holistic betterment of others.

Swamiji is a direct disciple of Jagadguru Shri Kripaluji Maharaj, the fifth original Jagadguru in Indian history. He has founded JKyog, a nonprofit organization dedicated to the betterment of humankind.  Swamiji is on a divine mission to help people lead truly successful lives. Using his educational and corporate background, he has an exceptional ability to teach the wisdom of the ancient scriptures in the context of our modern life. 

Sruthi Balamurali

Sruthi Balamurali is a renowned multi-instrumentalist, singer and Bharatanatyam dancer who is currently pursuing her undergraduate degree at the University of Toronto, in the Double Degree program. Being trained in Carnatic music by her father and guru Shri T.N. Balamurali, Sruthi is well versed on Vocal, Violin, Veena and Flute, as well as Western Classical Vocal, Flute and Piano. She has earned a large international audience across her social media platforms and earned millions of views for her productions on YouTube. She made her debut in the Indian film music industry as a violinist through music composer D Imman, for the hit film NAMMA VEETTU PILLAI. She has been honored with many awards including BEST INSTRUMENTALIST and INSTRUMENTAL SUPERSTAR 2020. Sruthi has performed on numerous stages in Canada, United States and India, and has received multiple awards from several media and organizations. Sruthi has had the privilege of sharing the stage with numerous legendary artists such as Dr. KJ Yesudas, P. Susheela and P. Unnikrishnan, to name a few, and has been commended by all of them for her outstanding talents.

Sparsh Rupesh Koyelot

Sparsh Rupesh Koyelot is a 10 year old child prodigy. Sparsh holds the world record for the youngest magician. He is also the title winner of Jadu Utsav 2018, Jantar Mantar 2018 and Taj Magic Festival 2018. He has also carved his name in the Indian Book of records for the most number of magicians performing together. He was accompanied by 199 co-magicians! At this tender age, Sparsh performs amazing magic tricks and puts everyone in awe. Till date he has witnessed 80 magic shows. Interestingly, his parents are magicians as well. His father Rupesh Koyelot and mother Kranti Koyelot perform extensively in various cities.

Devesh Mirchandani

Devesh Mirchandani is a star dancer, choreographer and a dance instructor. He is a disciple of Guru Pt.Birju ji Maharaj. Devesh is a Bollywood dance choreographer and a part of the Indian Film Dance Directors Association. Devesh is a perfectionist and is proficient in multiple dance styles such as Jazz, Kathak, Bollywood, Contemporary and Salsa. He has choreographed various masterpieces for reality shows such as Just Dance, Dance India Dance, and Dil Se Nache Indiawale. He has also conducted workshops at Dance With Madhuri and his choreographers are power-packed with superb moves and expressions. He also runs the Devesh Mirchandani Dance Academy at Mumbai. Devesh conducts online and in-person workshops. He has been in the dance industry now for 22 years and has also been teaching for 22 years. His every move is filled with energy, passion and precision. He is a Social Media influencer with a combined following of more than 2 million followers.
